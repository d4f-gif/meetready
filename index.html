MeetReady — swim meet qualifier

Enter a swimmer's USA Swimming details, pull their best times, then drop a meet sheet to see what they qualify for and which events to sign up for. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded, no account or AI needed.

1 · Who's swimming?

Don't know the ID? 🔍 Find a swimmer's ID on USA Swimming ↗

On that page, search the swimmer's name (works for girls and boys), open their profile, and copy the Member ID from the page's web address. Paste it above. Name, club and state here are optional — they're only used to confirm you matched the right swimmer.
