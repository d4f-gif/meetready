MeetReady — swim meet qualifier

Enter a swimmer's USA Swimming details, pull their best times, then drop a meet sheet to see what they qualify for and which events to sign up for. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded, no account or AI needed.

1 · Who's swimming?

The USA Swimming Member ID is the code in the swimmer's Times-page URL (find the swimmer on data.usaswimming.org or swimstandards.com and copy the ID from the address bar). Name, club and state are used only to confirm you've matched the right swimmer.

2 · Check a meet

Drop a meet-announcement PDF below (or tap to choose). It's parsed in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Current standing

Data: USA Swimming official Times API (best time per event and course, plus the official level USA Swimming assigns each swim). Levels come from USA Swimming; the "seconds to next cut" column fills in once a national motivational-standards table is added. Times are pulled live each time you look a swimmer up.
