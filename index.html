MeetReady — swim meet qualifier

Enter a swimmer's USA Swimming details, pull their best times, then drop a meet sheet to see what they qualify for and which events to sign up for. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded, no account or AI needed.

1 · Who's swimming?

Where do I find the Member ID?
  1. Open the USA Swimming Data Hub ↗.
  2. Sign in, or Create a Login (free). Searching a swimmer by name needs a signed-in account.
  3. Use Search → Athletes, type the swimmer's name (girls and boys both work), and open their page.
  4. Look at your browser's address bar. The Member ID is the code between /athlete/ and /best-times: data.usaswimming.org/search/athlete/9688AB9C4AFF4B/best-times
  5. Copy that code (here, 9688AB9C4AFF4B) and paste it above.

After you look it up, MeetReady shows the swimmer's name and club straight from USA Swimming, so you can confirm you've got the right one.
